Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PGDOST and our services.

What is PGDOST? ▼
PGDOST is a comprehensive property management platform designed specifically for PG and hostel owners. It helps you manage properties, residents, finances, and operations from a single dashboard.
How much does PGDOST cost? ▼
PGDOST offers flexible pricing plans starting from basic property management to premium enterprise solutions. We offer a free trial period to help you evaluate the platform. Contact our sales team for detailed pricing.
Is my data secure on PGDOST? ▼
Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption, regular backups, and follow international security standards. PGDOST is ISO 27001 certified and GDPR compliant to ensure your data is always protected.
Can I integrate PGDOST with my existing systems? ▼
Yes, PGDOST provides APIs and integrations with popular accounting software, payment gateways, and communication tools. Our technical team can help with custom integrations as needed.
What support options do you provide? ▼
We offer 24/7 customer support via email, phone, and live chat. Premium plans include dedicated account managers and priority support to ensure your success.
How long does it take to set up PGDOST? ▼
Most users can get started in under 30 minutes. Our onboarding process is straightforward, and our support team is available to guide you through setup and initial configuration.
